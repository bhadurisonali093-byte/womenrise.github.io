Why this space exists

Most of us were told we need a man, a marriage, or “society’s approval” to feel complete. This community exists to gently burn that script.

For real stories, not perfection

Cry about your boss, your in-laws, your ex, your kids, your confusion — without being called dramatic, ungrateful, or weak.

For women who want more

Whether “more” means a promotion, a divorce, a baby, a startup, or a quiet life — you get to define your own version of success.

For life’s invisible labour

Ageing parents, mental load at home, emotional caregiving… finally talk about the work no one claps for, but you still do.

How this will work

  1. 1 · Join the waitlist

    Tell us what you’re looking for — venting space, networking, single parent support, ageing parents, or all of it.

  2. 2 · Get matched to circles

    We’ll connect you to small peer groups that fit your life stage and boundaries (no random public drama).

  3. 3 · Rise together, at your pace

    Share stories, resources and referrals — or just listen quietly until you’re ready. No pressure to be “strong” all the time.
